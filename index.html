Memory Game

Instructions:

Your goal is to find all the matching pairs of cards.

  • Click on a card to flip it over and reveal its color.
  • Try to remember the colors and their positions.
  • Click on another card to flip it over.
  • If the two flipped cards have the same color, they will be slightly dimmed but stay visible, and you've found a match!
  • If the two flipped cards have different colors, they will be flipped back over.
  • Keep flipping cards and making guesses until you find all the matches.
  • Try to complete the game with the fewest number of guesses.
  • Click the "Level 1" button to begin the Level 1 game.
  • Click the "Level 2" button to begin the Level 2 game.
  • Click the "Reset" button to reset your scores.

Have fun and enjoy the Memory Game!
